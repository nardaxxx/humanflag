About the Association

Human Flag Association — Bellinzona, Ticino, Switzerland

Who we are

Human Flag Association (HFA) is a civil association constituted under Articles 60 et seq. of the Swiss Civil Code, based in Bellinzona, Canton Ticino. The Association was founded in [ANNO DI COSTITUZIONE] by Giovanni Nardacci. Its statutory purpose is the study and promotion of the practical effectiveness of International Humanitarian Law in environments mediated by autonomous and machine-speed systems, with particular reference to the recognition of surrender and hors de combat status.

HFA is a research initiative, not an operational or advocacy organisation. Its method is documentary: it publishes peer-reviewable working papers and technical notes under permanent identifiers, proposes candidate technical standards for independent validation, and addresses institutions through formal, verifiable questions anchored to obligations those institutions have already adopted.

HFA is independently funded. The research corpus was prepared without funding from any party with a commercial or governmental interest in the subject matter. Donations support publication costs, institutional accreditation and the maintenance of the Association's documentary infrastructure.
