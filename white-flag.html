The watcher changed, not the gesture

Why the white flag is no longer enough

The white flag has worked for centuries, because a human being sees it and understands it. With a machine we have no such certainty, because an autonomous system does not think the way we do, and no one can guarantee that it will recognize that gesture.

HF SIGNAL 01 exists to remove the doubt. It is a red flag with a white circle, a white light pulsing at 7 Hz, a radio tone at 144.0 MHz. The message is the same as the white flag, but in a form that sensors can detect clearly.

The time to surrender

Surrendering takes time, because you have to raise your hands, show the signal, let yourself be seen. A human being grants that time, a machine deciding in fractions of a second may not. If the system acts before the gesture, the right to surrender exists only on paper.

We are not asking for a new law, we are asking that the one that exists remain possible.
