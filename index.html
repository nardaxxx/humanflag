Why Human Flag

Every day we see people die without a voice, without being able to surrender, without the right to ask forgiveness. In a world where machines decide who lives and who dies, Human Flag stands as a moral symbol â€” a flag of survival and dignity.

What we do

We promote the right to surrender, even in the age of automation. We create documents, ethical codes, and recognition protocols. We raise awareness among institutions and the public, and we defend human dignity in times of war.

How we act

We created an official association, wrote a statute, launched this website, and published a manifesto. We contact organizations, build networks, and invite everyone to participate: share, speak, resist. The first step is to raise the flag.
